THE BRIEF

Make a 3-minute film. One camera. You talking. No script. No edits needed. Just you and the question.

— SHOOTING SCRIPT —
0:00 – 0:30
Introduce yourself. Not your name. Who you are right now — where you're at, what you're doing, why you're here.
0:30 – 1:30
Make the case for the shard. Why would it be worth it to you? What do you think is in it? What does owning a piece of truth mean to you, specifically?
1:30 – 2:15
Make the case against. Be honest. What's the doubt? What's the fear? Say it out loud.
2:15 – 2:45
Watch yourself. Rewind. Play it back. What do you see?
2:45 – 3:00
Final answer. Yes or no. If yes — say it like you mean it. Then submit.
