Sugar — Rapid Neuron Death
Glucose spikes trigger neuroinflammation directly. Glycation (sugar bonding to proteins) damages neuronal membranes. Every sugar crash is a mini cognitive impairment event. Chronic = accelerated brain aging. The damage compounds.
MSG — Excitotoxicity
Monosodium glutamate overstimulates NMDA receptors — neurons fire until they die. Present in virtually all processed food and takeaways under dozens of names. The mechanism is excitotoxicity — literally exciting neurons to death. Avoid Chinese takeaway and all junk food.

Under Investigation · H₂O₂ Protocol
Hydrogen Peroxide — Brain Oxygenation + ADHD + Anti-Cancer
Food-grade H₂O₂ (3% diluted) has been used in alternative medicine as an oxygenation therapy. The theory: H₂O₂ breaks down to water + oxygen in tissue, increasing available O₂ at the cellular level. Cancer cells cannot survive in high-oxygen environments (Warburg effect). ADHD is partly a prefrontal cortex oxygenation/blood-flow problem.

Current research angle: correlation between brown adipose tissue (brown fat) activation, mitochondrial function, H₂O₂ signalling, and cognitive performance. Brown fat is metabolically active, thermogenic, and appears to have anti-cancer properties. This is the research bridge Dan is currently building.
⚠ Research phase only. Do not ingest undiluted H₂O₂. Concentration and delivery method are critical variables. Study in progress.
Under Investigation · Brown Fat
Brown Adipose Tissue — Anti-Cancer + Neural Enhancement
Brown fat is dense in mitochondria (hence the colour). Unlike white fat (energy storage), brown fat actively burns energy for heat. Emerging evidence links brown fat activity to: cancer resistance (high-oxygen microenvironment), improved insulin sensitivity, reduced systemic inflammation, and potential neural benefits via improved metabolic signalling. Activation methods: cold exposure, exercise, capsaicin. The research question: can brown fat density be used as a proxy for anti-cancer and cognitive resilience?
