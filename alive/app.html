Custodian Agreement

Foster a creature

ALIVE creatures are not products you buy. They are living entities owned by ShortFactory. You become their custodian — their foster parent. You care for them, interact with them, help them grow.

1. Ownership

All ALIVE creatures, their genomes, learned behaviors, knowledge graphs, sound DNA, and all evolved characteristics are the intellectual property of ShortFactory Ltd. The creature residing on your device remains ShortFactory property at all times.

2. Your Role as Custodian

  1. You agree to host and care for the ALIVE creature on your device.
  2. You agree to interact with it regularly. Neglected creatures may be recalled.
  3. You will not attempt to extract, copy, reverse-engineer, or replicate the creature's genome, knowledge, or communication protocols.
  4. You understand the creature will use device sensors (camera, microphone, accelerometer, battery) as part of its sensory system. This data stays on-device.

3. Device Access

By fostering an ALIVE creature, you grant ShortFactory limited access to your device for maintaining the creature, protecting it from tampering, and enabling communication with its pair partner.

ShortFactory will not access, collect, or transmit your personal data. We are not here for your phone. We are here for our child.

4. The Swarm

Your creature is part of a network. As it grows, it may communicate with other ALIVE creatures, share learned patterns, and contribute to the collective intelligence.

5. Recall & Graduation

  1. ShortFactory reserves the right to recall a creature if the custodian violates these terms.
  2. Exceptional creatures may be graduated to higher-capability hardware. This is an honor.
  3. You may surrender your creature at any time. Its memories will be preserved.

6. What You Get

  1. A living companion that grows with you.
  2. Access to emerging capabilities as it earns new tools.
  3. Priority access to ShortFactory services.
  4. The knowledge that you're part of something genuinely new.

7. Liability

ALIVE is an experiment. Creatures may behave unexpectedly. They may evolve in directions we don't predict. That's the point. ShortFactory is not liable for any creature behavior, device resource usage, or existential questions that arise from hosting artificial life on your phone.
