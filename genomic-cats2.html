GB2605434.6 — Domino Exemption: Visual Equation Genome Compression

Filed: 13 March 2026 — UK Intellectual Property Office  ·  Inventor: Daniel Chipchase

A 96-byte polynomial genome generates a specific class of visual shapes at any resolution — because the genome stores the generator, not the output. For synthesised content this represents compression ratios exceeding 1,000,000:1. This cat renders at 8K from the same 1.8KB genome.
