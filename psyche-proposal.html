The asset

There is $10 quintillion safely swinging between Jupiter and Mars. Nobody owns it. No government can print more of it.

Conservative estimates place its elemental value at $10,000,000,000,000,000,000 — ten quintillion US dollars. That is more than one million times the total GDP of Earth.

It has been in its orbit for 4.5 billion years. It is going nowhere. NASA already has a mission en route. The question is not whether humanity reaches it. The question is who benefits when we do — and how we structure that wealth so it cannot be corrupted.

The answer is not to mine it all at once. The answer is to back a currency with it.
