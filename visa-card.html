📋 FCA Registration — Do You Need It?
The short answer: if you use Stripe Issuing or a BIN sponsor, they hold the FCA licence — you operate as their agent. You only need direct FCA registration if you're issuing e-money yourself.
✅
Stripe Issuing — Stripe is the FCA-regulated entity. You don't need your own licence.
✅
Railsr / Marqeta / Griffin — they are FCA-licensed. You register as their EMD Agent (simple, cheap).
⚠️
Direct FCA EMI licence — only if you want to hold customer funds yourself. Takes 12–18 months. Not needed yet.
