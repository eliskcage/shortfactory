02 — How To Build Your Funnel Video · The Proven Method
1
Film the Hook (Beats 1–2)
Your character, your danger prop. Doesn't need to be real — it needs to feel real. Dark room. Close crop. Countdown timer visible on screen. 3–8 seconds of pure tension. No talking.
↳ GTA: health bar draining. Squid Games: gun, guard, clock. Horror: jumper cables, chair.
2
Cut the Jumpscare (Beat 3)
Hard cut to impact frame at the exact moment the timer hits zero. Loud bang. Flash white. Then 2 frames of aftermath — floating objects, blood, gamepad, whatever fits your theme. This is the mechanism. The brain fires sideways.
↳ Don't overexplain it. 1 frame of shock is worth 10 seconds of buildup.
3
The Lift (Beat 4)
Audio reversal. The motivational hit — "Don't Let Your Dreams Be Dreams" or your theme's equivalent. The surreal aftermath lingers. Floating objects. Slow motion. This is where love triggers. From death comes meaning.
↳ The music does the work here. Choose it carefully — it's the emotional keystone.
4
Burn the Game (Beats 5–6)
4-digit code box appears on screen. Your TinyURL burns in large text. "Can you crack the code?" Text overlaid. Stay on this for 5+ seconds. Put TinyURL in bio. This is the bridge from view to click.
↳ Get your TinyURL from the panel below. It contains your ref code — every soul it funnels is credited to you.
5
Upload & Track
Hit the YouTube button below. OAuth connects your channel. Video uploads. Sonar tracks every soul your link funnels. Your dashboard shows earnings in real time.
↳ The code rotates every 24h — daily return traffic built in. Every day = a new game.
