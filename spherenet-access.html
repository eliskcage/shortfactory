PARAMETER PLAYBOOK — RESONANCE

RESONANCE controls the catch radius — how close a signal must be to activate a sphere. Too high: everything resonates with everything, no specificity. Too low: signals miss existing spheres and spawn endless new ones...


INDUSTRIAL WIRING — TICK ENGINE

The tick engine must be decoupled from signal ingestion. If you run tick() per signal you'll bottleneck at ~1k signals/sec. The fix: Redis queue for signals, Celery beat for ticks on a 500ms schedule...
